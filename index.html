● sonder.

poetry at golden hour

sonder.

words i write about her that i can't speak out loud.

soft fragments.
quiet universes.
verses held between sunlight and sleep.

poems

small universes, arranged in lines.

about

i write to make sense of what i feel.

sonder (n.) the quiet realization that everyone carries a universe within them.

this is a small window into mine—soft-spoken, dusk-colored, always almost saying what it means.

sonder © 2025 for you, isha
always.

songs for you

because every song sounds like you.
